Application Overview

The Bilingual Contract Generator is designed to streamline the creation and management of contracts in multiple languages. It integrates with Supabase for robust backend services including authentication and data storage.

Key features include:

  • Generating contracts in English and Spanish.
  • Managing parties and promoters involved in contracts.
  • A dashboard for analytics and audit logs.
  • Secure user authentication.
